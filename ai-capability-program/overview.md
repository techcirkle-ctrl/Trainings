# AI Capability Program — Overview

A 4-week structured program to turn senior leaders from AI-aware to AI-capable. By the end, each participant has **built a working agent for their own function and has a 90-day plan to lead AI in it** — not a certificate.

---

## Who it's for

5 nominated senior GCC leaders (MD / CPO / sales & business heads). They understand the AI shift is happening; they don't yet know how to incorporate it into their day-to-day operations. This is a **leadership cohort**, not a company-wide rollout.

## The outcome contract

Every participant leaves with:
- A **production-ready agent** deployed into their team's real workflow.
- A **90-day AI project plan** they sponsor and run.
- The capability to **scope, govern, and measure** AI initiatives in their function.

We measure capability, not completion — hours watched and badges earned don't count.

## Weekly rhythm

Built on a flipped-classroom model. Each week:

```
Pre-work (self-paced)  →  Session (5 hrs, trainer-led)  →  Homework (build/apply)
                          └── mid-week connect ──┘
```

| Phase | What |
|---|---|
| **Pre-work** | Self-paced courses + a PE reading. Arrive primed, not just exposed. |
| **Session** | 5 hrs in-person, trainer-led. Debrief → teach → build. |
| **Mid-week connect** | 45–60 min group build-clinic between session and homework deadline. Bring blockers, get unblocked live. Same ritual every week (see below). |
| **Homework** | Build or apply. Produces one concrete artifact per week. |

## The 4-week arc

| Week | Theme | Verb | Artifact produced |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | AI Foundations | Understand | 2-page architecture doc (problem mapped across the AI stack) |
| **2** | Agentic Deep Dive | Decode | Working agent (from spec sheet) + 1-page design doc |
| **3** | Hands-On Building | Practice | Capstone v1 prototype + spec + colleague feedback |
| **4** | Build & Lead | Deploy | Production agent + 30-day deployment + 90-day project plan |

Each week's detail lives in [`weeks/`](weeks/).

## Mid-week connect & support system

The client-requested support layer. Same shape every week, so it's described once here rather than repeated per week:

- **Mid-week build clinic** — 45–60 min video call, ~3 days after each session (e.g. session Mon → clinic Wed/Thu → homework due before next session). Open office-hours format: each leader brings a blocker, shares screen, gets unblocked. Recorded for anyone who can't attend.
- **Async doubt channel** — standing Teams/WhatsApp group + a shared doc. Questions answered anytime on business days, not only in the clinic.
- **Peer buddies** — pairs from Week 2 for peer review between sessions.

## After the 4 weeks

2 fortnightly 1-hr check-ins with the trainer to pressure-test the 90-day plans against real results and ensure AI practices land in the workflow. Validated program then scales to the wider GCC.

## Tooling

Claude as the AI engine; agents built in Claude Projects (no-code). Concepts retain full technical depth (agent lifecycle, tool use, MCP, multi-agent orchestration). The client may bring its own tool preferences — these are our recommendations.
