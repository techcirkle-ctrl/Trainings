# Week 1 — Homework: Map the Stack
**Time:** ~3 hrs

## Task
Take a real, complex problem from your function. Decompose it across the AI stack: which parts need prompts alone? RAG? Agents? Multi-agent?

Craft 3 production-quality prompts for the LLM-only parts and test them on Claude. Spec the RAG and agent components.

## Output
A 2-page architecture document showing how you'd solve the whole problem across the stack.
