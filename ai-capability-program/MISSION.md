# Mission

Grounds all teaching. Every lesson, demo, and exercise ties back to a mission below. (Operationalizes the Week-0 intake call into the teaching model's `MISSION` format.)

## Program mission

Turn 5 nominated senior GCC leaders from **AI-aware → AI-capable**. Each leaves with a production-ready agent deployed in their team's real workflow and a 90-day AI plan they personally sponsor. Capability is evidenced by what they ship, not by a certificate.

## Per-leader mission (captured in the Week-0 intake call)

One block per leader. Fill from the 15–30 min intake. This drives tailored Week-2 spec sheets, demos, and worked examples — and sets each leader's zone of proximal development.
